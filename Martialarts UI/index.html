*** One of the leading martial art academies ***

Our School is highly regarded for its fully structured belt grading syllabus, taking children and adult students from absolute beginners up to black belt level and beyond. We are different from harder and more external styles as our training syllabus also develops internal energy which is incorporated into our teaching techniques.

Children and adults can learn the basics of how to defend themselves from their very first class. Our classes will also help to improve fitness, confidence, coordination, mental and physical health as well as self-defence techniques.

All of our instructors are certified, fully safeguarding trained, Disclosure Barring Service (DBS) checked and fully insured.
